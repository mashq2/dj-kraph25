About

DJ Kraph is a Nairobi-based DJ and music producer known for his genre-blending sets that fuse Afro-house, Hip-hop, and Electronic music into high-energy, crowd-first performances. With over 1.2 million total plays and 150+ premium mix drops, he has built a loyal following across Kenya and beyond — consistently delivering sets that keep every room locked in from the first drop to the last fade.

His approach centres on storytelling through sound: reading the energy of the room, shaping smooth transitions, and blending frequencies with precision to create an unmistakable vibe.
