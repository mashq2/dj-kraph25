❓ FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase a mix?

Simply click on any mix you like, add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay using M-Pesa (Kenya), Credit/Debit Card, or PayPal. After payment is confirmed, your mix will be available for download immediately.

Can I preview the mix before buying?

Yes! Each mix has a 30-second preview available. Click the preview button on the mix card to listen before you purchase. This will help you decide if it's what you're looking for.

How do I download my purchased mixes?

After successful payment, you'll receive a download link via email. You can also access all your downloads from your account dashboard. Your purchases are stored in your library and available anytime.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa (for Kenya), Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard via Stripe), and PayPal for international payments. All payments are secure, encrypted, and processed instantly.

Can I create and share playlists?

Yes! Once you purchase mixes, you can create custom playlists and organize them however you like. You can also share playlists with other users using a unique share link.

What is a subscription plan?

Our subscription plans give you unlimited access to all mixes for a monthly fee. Choose between Basic (KSh 1,999/month) and Premium (KSh 3,999/month) plans. Cancel anytime.

How do I become an affiliate?

Interested in earning commission? Join our Affiliate Program and earn 15-20% commission on every sale you refer. Sign up in your account settings and start sharing your unique referral link.

Can I leave reviews for mixes?

Absolutely! After purchasing a mix, you can leave a review with a star rating and comment. Your feedback helps other users make better decisions and helps artists improve their work.

What is the wishlist feature?

The wishlist lets you save mixes you're interested in without buying them immediately. You can access your wishlist anytime and get notified when prices change or new mixes are added.

How do I contact support?

For any issues or questions, email us at support@djkraph.com or use the contact form on our website. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard SSL encryption and PCI DSS compliance to protect all payment information. Your data is never stored unencrypted and is handled with the utmost security.

Can I refund my purchase?

Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase if you haven't downloaded the mix. Once downloaded, refunds are not available as per our digital goods policy. Contact support for assistance.
